Frequently Asked Questions

Creating a FAQ for a company involves addressing common questions and concerns potential clients might have

1. What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is flexible and depends on the services required, the scope of the project, and the level of ongoing support needed. We offer different packages and can provide a customized quote after understanding your requirements.

2. Can you manage our social media accounts?

Yes, we offer comprehensive social media management services including content creation, posting, community management, and social media advertising.

3. How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy and it typically takes 3-6 months to start seeing significant results. However, this can vary based on the competitiveness of your industry and the current state of your website.

4. What is PPC and how does it work?

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is an advertising model where you pay each time someone clicks on your ad. It is a way to buy visits to your site rather than earning them organically. Google Ads is a popular PPC platform.

5. Do you offer customized marketing plans?

Yes, we tailor our marketing strategies to meet the specific needs and goals of each client. Our approach is customized based on your unique business objectives and target audience.
